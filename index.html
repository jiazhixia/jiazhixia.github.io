Short Bio

Xia jizhi, Ph.D., associate professor, works in the computer department of the school of information science and engineering, central south university. The research field includes data visualization analysis, computer graphics and human-computer interaction. In 2011, he received his doctoral degree from nanyang technological university in Singapore. He received his bachelor's degree and master's degree in computer science from zhejiang university in 2005 and 2008 respectively. He has presided over the national natural science fund youth fund project, the ministry of education's doctoral fund program and the hunan science and technology project. More than 30 academic papers were published in IEEE VAST, IEEE TVCG, ACM MM, ACM SIGCHI, IEEE TCSVT, CAD, etc. In 2017, the group in the international top meeting IEEE visualization VAST (TVCG Track) published an article, the subspace of high-dimensional data clustering, clustering the pre-review of manifold problems are studied, implements the central south university at the top of the visualization of the international conference.
